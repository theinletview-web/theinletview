About Inlet Haven

A peaceful retreat in Port Alice, BC.

Inlet Haven B&B offers a quiet and comfortable escape surrounded by the natural beauty of Port Alice. Our goal is to provide a relaxing home-away-from-home experience for every guest.

Whether you're visiting for outdoor adventure, a weekend getaway, or a peaceful retreat, we welcome you with warm hospitality and personalized care.
